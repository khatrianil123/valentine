Still You. Always You. ❤️

No matter how far we drifted, my heart still remembers the way you smiled at me.
Every place feels incomplete without you standing beside me.
Time passed, but my love for you never learned how to fade.
I miss the little moments — the laughs, the looks, the quiet understanding.
I’ve grown, I’ve learned, and I now understand what I almost lost.
You were never just a chapter. You were the whole story to me.
If I could go back, I wouldn’t change loving you — only how I handled it.
My heart still chooses you — quietly, deeply, and sincerely.
If you ever feel the same, let’s not rewrite the past — let’s build something stronger.
Made with love, hope, and a heart that still believes in us ❤️
